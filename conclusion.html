Conclusion

In this course, we learned the important architectures and concepts in software design and applied them in a fitness app development. Through the process of developing this app, we applied SOLID and GRASP into our development. The final outcome of this course is an Android App which allowed the users to create and track their fitness workout and goals. We gained experience in Android App development through this course and learned the importance of teamwork. We learned how to collaborate with different people with different background and skills. We also enhanced our self-learning skill when we faced the challenges. Overall, this is an unforgettable summer experience for everyone in out=r team.
