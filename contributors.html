Contributors

Arnav Arora

Arnav contributed to the Personal Information Screen functionality, Workout Tracking screen UI and functionality, and aided in comprehensive testing of various aspects of HealthTrack's functionality. He also contributed to analysis write-ups of the various design principles and patterns utilized in HealthTrack's codebase.

Boyu Cheng

Boyu contributed to the initial creation of screens and the UI and functionality behind the Calorie screen. His responsibities also included comprehensive unit testing on the Workout Plan and Community Challenge functionalities with Miranda and work on various Use Case Scenarios for the app with Sumukh.

Miranda Dominguez

Miranda contributed to the app navigation bar and Personal Information screen. Her responsibilities also included comprehensive unit testing on the Workout Plan and Community Challenge functionalities alongside Boyu. She also contributed to the Analysis and Design phase of HealthTrack's development by producing a Use Case Diagram and Domain Class Diagram with Ashley.

Sumukh Paspuleti

As Team Lead, Sumukh worked hard to ensure the team met important deadlines and allocated responsibilities amongst team members. As a developer, he contributed to the Sign-Up/Authentication, Personal Information, Workout Plans, and Community Challenges screens. He also contributed to the Analysis and Design phase of HealthTrack's Development by producing a Design Class Diagram, Sequence Diagram, and Use Case Scenarios with Boyu and Tejes.

Tejes Prabakaran

Tejes contributed to the UI and functionality of the Workout Tracking and Workout Plan screens. His work on the Workout Plan tracking was source of inspiration for the Community Challenges UI and functionality. He also contributed to the Analysis and Design phase of HealthTrack's development by producing a Sequence Diagram with Sumukh.

Ashley Vaia

Ashley contributed to the UI and functionality for the Login/Authentication, Calorie, and Community Challenges screens on HealthTrack. Her responsibilities also included writeups analyzing various design patterns implemented in the codebase. She also contributed to the Analysis and Design phase of HealthTrack's development by producing a Use Case Diagram and Domain Class Diagram with Miranda.
